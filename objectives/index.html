Our Objectives

  • To improve rural livelihood and economic prosperity by strengthening the capacity of primary women cooperatives.
  • Advocacy to increase participation of social entrepreneurs in policy making process in all levels of government (Local, province and federal)
  • To increase access to education, health, nutrition, insurance, input supply, market and access to finance through the women empowerment social capital development, social transformation and awareness raising.
  • To facilitate producer for consumer friendly quality production, processing and marketing of Agriculture-livestock products.
  • Develop leadership of women involve in agriculture and livestock business by forming group and networks to develop social entrepreneurship and improve livelihood
